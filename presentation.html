Theorem 2

Game-Theoretic Optimal Degree

✅

degree ≤ d* → Full Rewards

Validator receives proportional rewards from all allocated services. Optimal participation.

⛔

degree > d* → Zero Rewards

Over-allocating beyond the threshold earns nothing. Hard enforcement via smart contract.

Result
Validators naturally converge to exactly d*. Neither under-allocating (lost rewards) nor over-allocating (zero rewards) is rational.
Reward vs. Restaking Degree
Theorem 2 proves a Nash equilibrium at degree d*. Validators self-organize to the optimal degree without central coordination. Implemented in the distribute_rewards instruction.
